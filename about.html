At Our CORE

Core Accounting is a professional accounting firm dedicated to simplifying financial management for small and medium-sized businesses across Jamaica. We provide clear, reliable, and growth-oriented accounting solutions that help business owners make confident decisions.

With over 20 years of combined experience, our team delivers expertise in accounting, tax, audit preparation, and financial advisory — ensuring accuracy, compliance, and strategic insight at every step.

At the heart of Core Accounting is a belief that finance should be transparent and stress-free. We go beyond numbers, helping our clients understand the story behind their finances so they can focus on what truly matters — growing their business.
